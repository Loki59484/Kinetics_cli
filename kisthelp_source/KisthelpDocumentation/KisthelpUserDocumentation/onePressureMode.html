V.1 Description of KiSThelP Pressure Bar: single pressure mode
 

 KiSThelP allows the calculation of :
               - rate constants
               - equilibrium constants
               - thermodynamic properties for an atom or a molecule
       at the pressure you want.
 

        This tools bar is located at the bottom of the central KiSThelP window.
        Clicking on the first radio button, the user can change pressure very easily.
