V.2 Description  of  KiSThelP  Pressure  Bar: pressure range mode
 

        KiSThelP allows the calculation of :
             - rate constants
             - equilibrium constants
             - thermodynamic properties for an atom or a molecule
       within a pressure range (results displayed as a graph).
 

        This tools bar is located at the bottom of the central KiSThelP window.
        Click on the second radio bouton to activate the range pressure mode.
