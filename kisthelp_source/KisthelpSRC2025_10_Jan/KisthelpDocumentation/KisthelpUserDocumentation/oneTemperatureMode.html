IV.1 Description of  KiSThelP Temperature Bar: single temperature mode

 

        KiSThelP allows the calculation of :
               - rate constants
               - equilibrium constants
               - thermodynamic properties for an atom or a molecule
       at the temperature you want.
 

        This tools bar is located at the top of the central KiSThelP window.
        Clicking on the first radio button, the user can change temperature very easily.
