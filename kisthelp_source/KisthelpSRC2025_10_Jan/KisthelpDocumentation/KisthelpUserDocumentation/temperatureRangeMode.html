IV.2 Description of  KiSThelP  Temperature  Bar: temperature range mode

 

        KiSThelP allows the calculation of :
             - rate constants
             - equilibrium constants
             - thermodynamic properties for an atom or a molecule
       within a temperature range (results displayed as a graph).
 

        This tools bar is located at the top of the central KiSThelP window.
        Click on the second radio bouton to activate the range temperature mode.
